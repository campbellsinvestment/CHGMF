Frequently Asked Questions

How can I make a donation?

You can make a donation online through our Ways to Give page, by mail, or by calling our donation hotline. All major credit cards and PayPal are accepted.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes! CHGMF is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for your records.

How do I volunteer?

Visit our volunteer opportunities page or contact us directly. We offer both local and international volunteer positions with various time commitments.

Where does my donation go?

92% of donations go directly to programs. We maintain transparency with detailed annual reports showing exactly how funds are allocated across our various initiatives.

Ready to Make a Difference?

Whether you want to donate, volunteer, or partner with us, we're here to help you get started.
