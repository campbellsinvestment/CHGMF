Who We Are

Caring Hearts Global Mission Foundation is a Non-Profit Service Learning Entity that strongly believes in our vision. The Caring Hearts Global Mission Foundation aims to advocate for improved human living conditions, support global health promotion and enhance service learning.

Our Foundation

Vision

Improving lives one mission at a time.

Mission

Optimally improve the health, living conditions and general education needs of vulnerable/underserved populations globally.

Goals

Our goals encompass three main themes: improved human living conditions, global health promotion and service learning—achieved through community engagement, participation, empowerment and collaboration.
